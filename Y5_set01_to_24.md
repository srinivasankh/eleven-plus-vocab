# Set 1 Vocabulary List

## 1. Ajar

**Type:** Adjective

**Meaning:** slightly open i.e. a door

**Synonyms:** open, unlatched, unshut

**Antonyms:** closed, shut

**Example Sentences:**
1. She left the kitchen door ajar so the cat could come and go.
2. The window was slightly ajar, letting in a cool night breeze.
3. He noticed the cookie jar lid was ajar and knew his sister had taken one.
4. Through the ajar curtains, she could see the twinkling city lights.
5. The book was left ajar on the table, marking the page he had finished reading.
